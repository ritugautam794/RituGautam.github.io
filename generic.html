THE Data Scientist

Welcome! I'm Ritu Gautam, a Data Scientist with a passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights. My journey in the world of data began with a foundation in Computer Science and Engineering, recently culminating in a Master's in Management Analytics from the prestigious Smith School of Business at Queen's University.

With 6 years of combined professional experience in data science and software engineering, I excel in transforming complex data into actionable insights. My expertise spans data analysis, machine learning, and predictive forecasting techniques. This diverse skill set enables me to develop high-quality software solutions that drive data-driven decision-making to solve complex business challenges, as demonstrated in my projects ranging from optimizing marketing strategies to forecasting global temperature trends.

When I'm not immersed in the world of data, I’m out exploring the great outdoors as an avid hiker, always on the hunt for my next adventure. Swimming is my escape, providing a refreshing balance to my busy life. In the kitchen, I unleash my creativity by experimenting with international flavors and hosting lively dinner parties. If you love food and data as much as I do, let’s connect and explore together!
